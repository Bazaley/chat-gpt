ChatGPT does not give effective results to everyone WHY? 🤔

📉 Limited training data: ChatGPT's ability to generate text is heavily reliant on the quality and quantity of training data it receives.

🤷‍♂️ Ambiguity in language: Language is inherently ambiguous, and ChatGPT's interpretation of a prompt can be influenced by the ambiguity of language.

🧠 Lack of domain knowledge: ChatGPT has a vast knowledge base, but it may not always have specific domain knowledge that is relevant to a marketer's needs.
